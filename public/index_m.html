Welcome to L'Impression

Respecting ingredients from local terroir and the heritage of Korean gastronomy,
we apply our genuine understanding of aging and fermentation to offer you a unique contemporary French Cuisine.

By sharing our philosophy, inspiration and passion through our creations, we hope to provide you with a memorable dining
experience.

Chef Allen Suh

While studying hospitality management at UNLV, Allen becomes fascinated by the process of creating something with his own hands in the kitchen.

After gaining experience in few renowned Michelin starred restaurants, Allen starts working at <Eleven Madison Park> under Daniel Humm in 2013, where his passion and maturation leads him to blossom as a sous chef.

Returning home to Korea after 17 years of experience on foreign soil, Allen finally gets a chance to showcase his own creation at <L’Impression>

UNLV에서 호텔경영을 공부하던 중에 우연히 요리를 접하게 되며, 온전히 내 손으로 무엇인가를 만들어내는 과정에 매료되어 요리사가 되었습니다.

세계적인 수준의 미슐랭 가이드 레스토랑들에서 조금씩 경험을 쌓고, <Eleven Madison Park>라는 큰무대에 2013년에 합류하여 Daniel Humm의 아래에서 그가 가진 열정과 경험을 인정받고 수셰프로서의 역량을 펼치게 됩니다.

한국을 떠난지 17년이 지난 후 모국으로 복귀하여, <L’Impression>에서 본인만의 요리를 펼치고자 합니다.
